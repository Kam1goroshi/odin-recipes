Flatbread Lasagna

Ingredients

  • 1 onion, large
  • 1 green bell pepper
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 4-5 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 pinch granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon oregano
  • 1 teaspoon rosemary
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • 1 kilo ground pork
  • salt
  • pepper
  • 400g canned tomatoes
  • 1 heaping tablespoon(s) tomato paste
  • 200g water
  • 250g cream cheese
  • 500g pita breads, large
  • 500g mixed cheese

To serve:

  • rocket
  • cherry tomatoes
  • olive oil

Recipe

  1. Place a large frying pan over high heat and add 2 tablespoons of olive oil.
  2. Cut the onion into small pieces and add them to the pan.
  3. Cut the peppers into cubes and add them to the pan. Add the sugar, oregano, rosemary, thyme, and sauté them for 1-2 minutes until caramelized.
  4. Add the ground pork and sauté it for 8-10 minutes until browned and cooked through.
  5. Add salt, pepper, the canned tomatoes, tomato paste, water, cream cheese, and mix. Remove the pan from the heat and set it aside.
  6. Preheat the oven to 180ο C (350ο F) set to fan.
  7. Place one pita in a 28 cm baking pan and drizzle it with 1 teaspoon of olive oil. Add 1/5 of the filling, 1/6 of the cheese, and cover with another pita. Follow the same process for all the ingredients.
  8. Spread the remaining cheese over the last pita and bake the lasagna for 15-20 minutes.
  9. Serve with rocket, cherry tomatoes, and olive oil.
