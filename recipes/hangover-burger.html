Hangover Burger

Ingredients

For the burger patties:

  • 50 g butter, ice-cold
  • 500 g ground beef
  • salt
  • pepper
  • 8 slices cheddar
  • 8 slices bacon

To assemble:

  • 2 burger buns
  • 2 tablespoons BBQ sauce
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tablespoon(s) olive oil
  • 2 slices tomato
  • 100 g iceberg lettuce
  • 2 tablespoon(s) mayonnaise
  • salt
  • pepper

To serve:

  • pickled cucumber
  • french fries

Recipe

For the burger patties:

  1. Place a frying pan over high heat.
  2. Sprinkle the butter over the ground beef, add salt and pepper, and knead well. Divide into 4 patties, about 120-140 g each, and put them in the hot pan.
  3. Press them with a spatula and cook them for 1-2 minutes. Flip the patties over and put 2 slices of cheddar on top of each one.
  4. Cook them for 1-2 minutes. Optionally, you can add 50 g of water into the pan, cover it with a lid, and cook for 30 seconds. Remove the patties and set them aside.
  5. Place another frying pan on heat and add the bacon. Cook it for 2-3 minutes on both sides and place it on top of each patty.
  6. Cut the burger buns in half and put them in the pan. Let them turn nicely golden and remove from the heat.

To assemble:

  1. Place a frying pan over medium heat and add the olive oil. Add the eggs and fry them. Remove and set aside.
  2. Spread the bbq sauce over the bottom buns and place 2 patties with the cheese and the bacon on top.
  3. Place one egg and 1 tomato slice on top of the patties.
  4. Spread the mayonnaise over the top buns, add the iceberg lettuce, and place the top buns to assemble the burgers.
  5. Serve with the pickled cucumbers and French fries.
