Carbonara Pizza

Ingredients

For the bechamel sauce:

  • 70 g butter
  • 1 clove(s) of garlic
  • 70 g all-purpose flour
  • 500 ml milk, 3,5% fat
  • salt
  • pepper
  • 1/3 teaspoon nutmeg

For the pizza:

  • 8 slices sandwich bread
  • 300g mixed cheese
  • 150g champignon mushrooms, canned
  • 8 slices bacon

Recipe

For the bechamel sauce:

  1. Add the butter into a saucepan over medium heat and let it melt.
  2. Finely chop the garlic and add it to the saucepan along with the flour.
  3. Whisk well for 1-2 minutes, until the roux is homogenized and cooked.
  4. When it’s ready (it will have a velvety texture and it won’t stick to your hands anymore), wrap it in plastic wrap so that it doesn’t dry, and let it rest for 30-60 minutes.
  5. Add the milk, salt, pepper, the nutmeg, and whisk for 3-4 minutes or until the bechamel sauce thickens.
  6. Remove the saucepan from the heat and set it aside until needed.

For the pizza:

  1. Preheat the oven to 200° C (390° F) set to fan.
  2. Place 4 sandwich bread slices on your worktop, spread half of the bechamel sauce over them, sprinkle with half of the cheese, add half of the mushrooms, and place half of the bacon slices on top.
  3. Cover with the other 4 bread slices, creating 4 sandwiches.
  4. Cut the sandwiches diagonally and place one piece next to the other in a baking pan lined with parchment paper to create a round pizza crust.
  5. Use a spoon to spread the rest of the bechamel sauce over the whole surface of the pizza.
  6. Sprinkle with the remaining mixed cheeses, the mushrooms, and the bacon.
  7. Bake in the oven for 15-20 minutes.
  8. Remove the baking pan from the oven and serve.
